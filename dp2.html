      Solitude and reflection are important to grow because you can't improve if you don't know what you are doing wrong and how to do it better. Solitude is required because you need time without outside influence to think for yourself, and your own thinking is what helps your brain get better at thinking. Learning is not the memorising of facts but the teaching of the mind to think. You get a better understanding of the facts while discussing them in a group, but the teaching of your mind to think happens alone.
