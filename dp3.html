On the left is a link to my wildlife portfolio, a catalogue of species in Colorado, with information such as habitat, range, descriptions, population behaviour, etc. These two projects are directly about the natural world and our place in it.
      Understanding the natural world is important because we live in it, depend on it, and if not careful, will destroy it. The natural world is literally everywhere and is the very reality in which we live. Understanding and studying the natural world will allow all living things to flourish. There is a reason the best inventions are biomimetic. A good understanding of the natural world is vital to living in it; you can't be conscientious if you don't understand what you are doing.
